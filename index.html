Music Streaming & Discovery

Global Giants

Spotify Sweden/Global

www.spotify.com
The defining force of global streaming. Its data-driven recommendation system and global charts serve as the modern music industry's barometer.

Apple Music USA/Global

music.apple.com
Pioneer of high-quality audio (lossless, spatial audio) with seamless Apple ecosystem integration and a massive global user base.

YouTube Music USA/Global

music.youtube.com
Features the most extensive "unofficial" library (live performances, covers, remixes), dominating visual music consumption and long-tail content discovery.

Deezer France/Global

www.deezer.com
Highly influential French streaming service in European and Latin American markets, renowned for its "Flow" personalized recommendations and Hi-Fi audio service.

Independent & Hi-Fi Options

Bandcamp USA/Global

bandcamp.com
The digital home and core business platform for independent musicians worldwide. Direct artist support and the best channel for discovering cutting-edge, niche music.

SoundCloud Germany/Global

soundcloud.com
The "sketchbook" and social platform for emerging musicians and electronic producers, where countless trends are born.

Tidal Norway/Global

tidal.com
Designed specifically for audiophiles and listeners seeking ultimate sound quality, offering master-quality audio streaming.

Qobuz France/Global

www.qobuz.com
Another platform focused on high-resolution audio streaming and downloads, beloved by classical and jazz enthusiasts.

Asian Markets

QQ Music China

y.qq.com
Flagship product of Tencent Music Entertainment Group (TME), featuring the most extensive exclusive copyright library and massive user base in China.

NetEase Cloud Music China

music.163.com
Distinguished in the Chinese market by its unique community culture, user comments, and precise algorithmic recommendations.

Kugou Music China

www.kugou.com
Also under TME, with deep penetration in lower-tier markets and a massive user base. Its live streaming and karaoke features are extremely popular.

Melon South Korea

www.melon.com
South Korea's most dominant music platform, with its "Melon Top 100" chart being the ultimate standard for measuring success in the K-Pop industry.

KKBOX Taiwan/Asia

www.kkbox.com
Important streaming service in the Chinese music sphere with deep roots in Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Southeast Asian markets.

JioSaavn India

www.jiosaavn.com
India's leading streaming service, offering vast collections of Bollywood hits and regional language music from across South Asia.
